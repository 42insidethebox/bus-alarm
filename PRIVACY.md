# BusBell Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

BusBell does not create user accounts and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Timetables, preferences, backups, and saved locations are stored locally on the user's device.

BusBell requests notification permission to deliver departure reminders. It requests foreground location permission only when a user saves a place or refreshes place-aware reminders. Location coordinates are processed on-device and are not transmitted to BusBell or third parties.

Deleting the application removes its locally stored data. Users can create a portable JSON backup from the Settings screen; the user controls where that copied data is stored or shared.

Questions about this policy can be directed to the support contact published with the App Store or Play Store listing.
