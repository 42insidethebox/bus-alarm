# BusBell brand

## Positioning

BusBell is the calm, private reminder for departures people already know. The identity combines a front-facing bus and a ringing bell: literal enough to understand instantly, distinctive enough to recognize at small app-icon sizes.

## Voice

Clear, reassuring, concise, and lightly playful. Prefer “Your bus is in 5 minutes” over technical scheduling language. Avoid anxiety, surveillance language, and exaggerated reliability claims.

## Palette

- Forest: `#216A4A` — primary brand and transit confidence
- Cream: `#F5F7F2` — warm background and icon foreground
- Amber: `#F4B942` — bell, urgency, and highlights
- Ink: `#17211B` — primary text
- Muted: `#66736B` — secondary text

## App icon

The master mark is in `assets/icon.png`. Keep the complete bus, bell, and both ringing lines visible. Do not add text, recolor individual parts, rotate it, or place other elements inside the mark's clear space.

Generated with the built-in image-generation tool using this production prompt:

> Create a memorable minimal symbol combining a small public bus front silhouette with a ringing bell, merged into one simple app icon. Use a deep forest green background, warm cream bus, and restrained golden amber bell. Premium flat, geometric, Swiss-transit-inspired, centered and legible at 32 pixels. No words, letters, numbers, gradients, mockup, device, or watermark.
